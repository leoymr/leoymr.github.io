Public Service

Virtual Service Booth

A virtual office of citizen care is a kind of closed kiosk, to preserve the privacy and confidentiality of the users, equipped with all the necessary elements such as touch screen with virtual keyboard, a high-definition video display and high quality audio for teleconferencing, a printer and a high definition camera scanner. The interested people can carry out any procedure like request a certificate or make a complaint without having to move to an OAC in person.
Reference: http://www.digitalavmagazine.com/en/2013/02/25/barcelona-estrena-una-oficina-virtual-de-atencion-ciudadana-con-telepresencia/

Telecare

The quickest emergency response service for people who are elderly, with disabilities or dependent on others. It works as simple as this: a device is installed in the individual’s home and directly connected to a Call Centre, which can be contacted at the simple press of a button. The Call Centre contacts not only emergency services, but family member and designated contacts. The total was 70,000 people using these services in Barcelona.
Reference: http://www.mobility-services.in.tum.de/?p=2237

mobileID

idBCN is a digital identity system provided by the Barcelona City Council as a free public service, which allows citizens to identify themselves remotely and securely to a number of digital services by means of the digital ID in their mobile phone. Reference: http://www.mobileid.cat/en/mobile-id-for-citizens/

Environment

Smart Rubbish Collection

The pneumatic and selective waste collection system minimizes noise pollution from the traditional waste collection methods and improves quality of urban spaces as waste containers disappear from the streets. The system consists of a network of fixed collection points strategically distributed, where the users deposit their waste allowing the separation of waste fractions. The compact drop-off containers have a subterranean vacuum network through the pipes, sucking up trash below the ground, which decreases noise pollution made by trash trucks and keeps the public space and stench clear.

Smart Watering

Smart irrigation system gather information about humidity, salinity, temperature, wind and several other factors through sensors that automatically regulate the amount of water and time by means of a program that can be managed with computers, smartphones and tablets. Besides, it will stop watering during bad weather to save water source.

Smart Lighting

More efficient lighting using LED technology is being installed in Barcelona to reduce cost and pollution. These lights optimize energy and use a smart function: it activates when detecting motion, but also gathers environmental information, humidity, temperature, pollution, and noise.

Mobility

Bicing

With more than 420 stations and 6,000 bicycles circulating, Bicing is a sustainable and economical form of transport, designed for citizens to travel short distances without consuming any energy.

People can check the position of stations, the number of aviliale bicycles and payment recording through mobile app, making it easier to plan a route.
Reference: https://www.bicing.cat/

Smart Traffic Light System

Smart traffic light system was designed for helping blind people to use pedestrian crossings. When the light turns to green, it will emit special sound to remind the blind people. The system was further developed to help fire engine convoys reach their destination. It can manage the traffic light system to turn green for the route of the fire trucks until they have reached the site. Afterwards, the traffic light system returns to standard operation. This way the fire brigade reaches the destination safely and as quickly as possible.

Micro Platform for goods Dilivery

In order to make deliveries quicker and more sustainable, the City Council of Barcelona has promoted a pilot project using electric tricyclesIts. It just look like a mini loading and unloading dock. The trucks and vans carrying goods into the Old Town will be unloaded on specific position and from there, electric tricycles will deliver to the neighbourhoods. This solution has been chosen because the electric tricycles can carry up to 180 kilos of cargo, offering accessibility in a more efficient and time-saving way, as well as stop delivery vehicles blocking the streets.

Research & Innovation

Open Data BCN

This is public information that is available for everybody to reuse it however they like. Citizens, businesses, and other institutions can use the info such as election results, population, public facilities, or economy to generate new services instead of starting from scratch.

City OS

City OS is an open system that will enable the integration of all the current municipal information systems (local population register, taxes, permits etc.,) with the new public management systems (mobility, energy, resilience, noise etc.) and also with the systems of private service operators and suppliers (water, energy etc.,) and all the public players (social, cultural, education), by shaping the wide concept of Barcelona as a Smart City.

Telecommunication

BARCELONA WIFI

Barcelona WiFi is a Barcelona City Council service that enables you to connect to the Internet through WiFi access points, or hotspots, located in various municipal amenities and public access points. Barcelona WiFi is a free internet access network available to all citizens, an online and mobile network which facilitates fast and convenient information searches and the social use of the internet, adapting us to today's knowledge society. We provide Barcelona residents with the biggest, free public WiFi network in Spain and one of the biggest in Europe.

Smartquesinas

Intelligent furniture for the citizen in the Paseo de Gracia in Barcelona. The city of Barcelona already has eight new 'smartquesinas' located in Passeig de Gràcia bus stops, as part of the rehabilitation works of this emblematic Avenue of the city, offering interactive services to citizens.
Reference: http://www.digitalavmagazine.com/en/2014/12/29/smartquesinas-mobiliario-inteligente-para-el-ciudadano-en-el-paseo-de-gracia-de-barcelona/

Governance

Citizen’s Postbox

With the aim to have faster service delivery, Barcelona introduced this project. It works as a digital complaints log for its citizens. The city’s council is bound to attend to the complaints put forth by its’ citizens. The great thing about this service is that it reports the latest incidents in the city to the government in real time and it avoids tedious ways that waste time such as going to the city council office to report or issue a complaint which can take ones valuable time.

Barcelona Open Government

Barcelona open government is based on promoting a relationship about participation and working as a unit, between the city council and the citizens. The technological facilities are all about making a great change which will change the order of governing for people to that of governing with people. The change has led to a great outcome, it has developed the society and made a change in a more moderate manner in which they all want to participate in everything that affects their environment. Barcelona council is well aware of this situation and has decided to counter this conundrum by governing the city.
